Pasta

Description

This creamy pasta dish combines al dente spaghetti with a rich Alfredo sauce, perfect for a quick yet indulgent weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese, black pepper, and a pinch of salt. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens, about 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  6. Remove from heat, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve immediately.
