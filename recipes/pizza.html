Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza is a fun and customizable dish with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings. This recipe is great for pizza night with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or heated stone, and brush lightly with olive oil.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add pepperoni, bell peppers, and mushrooms.
  6. Sprinkle dried basil on top.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Slice and serve hot.
