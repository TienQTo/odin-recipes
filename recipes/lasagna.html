Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty meal is perfect for family gatherings or a cozy dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned, then drain excess fat. Add marinara sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta mixture and sprinkle mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella.
  6. Sprinkle remaining Parmesan on top, cover with foil, and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
